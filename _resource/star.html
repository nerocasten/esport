Tiểu Sử

Hai “Hai” Lam is a veteran mid laner, jungler, support and shot-caller. While Hai preferred assassin champions in the mid lane, he developed his own unique playstyle in the jungle and at support. When he returned to the mid lane for Cloud9 Challenger, he led the team through the 2017 NA LCS Spring Promotion Tournament to earn a spot in the big league.
Now, Hai will serve as the fearless captain for FlyQuest. With LemonNation and Balls there to back him up, it will be just like old times. But the game has changed since Hai last walked down mid lane in the NA LCS. Can he still measure up to the league's new brand of mid laner? Can he adjust to the 2017 Season's mid lane meta? If his mechanics are still up to par, his shotcalling can easily push FlyQuest over the hump and into the playoffs.
