The dream meme team is back for 2017 with some promising new additions. Although Cloud9 performed the best of any North American team at the 2016 World Championship, they weren’t satisfied with their quick exit in the quarterfinals.
Former Challenger scene standout Contractz has been handed the reigns to the jungle in his rookie season. The team also acquired Ray, Apex Gaming’s former playmaking top laner. If they can hone their raw talent with the guidance of the team’s veterans, Cloud9 could rise to new heights this year.
